## What we collect

- **Account and order information** you provide when you register, sign in, complete checkout, or contact us, such as your name, email, phone, and shipping address.

## Cookies and sessions

We use a secure **session cookie** when you sign in so the site can remember you on return visits. It is not exposed to page scripts the way ordinary cookies can be.
